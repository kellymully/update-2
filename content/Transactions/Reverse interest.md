 ### Reverse interest
 
 
 The **Reverse Interest** function allows the user to undo or reverse interest entries that were posted earlier when the computation or posting was erroneous. This ensures that member balances and reports remain accurate.

 ***Step 1: Open the reverse interest page***

 Go to the Operations menu, then click on the Reverse Interest sub menu.

 <!-- ![](/images/reverse.png) -->
 ![text](/images/reverse.png)

 ***Step 2: select an intrest posting to reverse***

 The user can view a list of interest rates or interest postings that were posted earlier and select one for reversal if the computation was erroneous.

Once the correct record has been selected, follow the on screen prompts to confirm and complete the reversal.
