  ### Fund withdrawals

  **Fund withdrawals** is the process by which an investor sells their units back to the fund manager to remove money from the investment. 

  **How to check the fund withdwawal listing**

  ***Step 1:***
  
Go to operations menu then click on the **Fund Withdrawal** sub-menu.


![](/images/opere.png)


***Step 2:***

A user can generate a list of withdrawals made from the fund.
